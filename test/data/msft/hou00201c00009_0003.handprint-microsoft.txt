Washinglow Jan 14th 1664
Dear Jur.
am very much obliged
by the prompt Rueduer of his
Twee and yourself us complying
with my request
I have received both the report
and the Tribune.
With cordial thanks
Songs Truly
Francis 1. Daich,
Clerk of Com. on tor. hel.
J. M. W. Serrurlou Esy
Avier. Freeomend Jug. Com.
n. y.
140 Second as. cor. of East gate.
