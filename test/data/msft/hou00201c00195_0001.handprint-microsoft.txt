Per Mer. Broadwater
(Days )
Every Child is allound
to go to the goverment school, but the colored
people can have seperate schools if they
desire in, and they have them in Chatham
and Dr leatherines, They were deceund by the
aboletinests, who told them they could not
School their children so will at the gomoment
Schools, and if they corned take them away,
they worked teach them for less .- and they
Charge them double. The colored child has
Just the same treatment in the goverment
schools as the white; there is no deception
about that
